Perimeter LED Screens

Our LED perimeter screens are used by sport clubs and companies that own sports infrastructures to be installed in stadiums, both outdoors and indoors. They are typically used in: stadiums, sports halls, racetracks, multi-purpose sports facilities, racetracks and other professional sports applications.

Thanks to the modular system our perimeter screen can cover any length, depending on the sport and your needs. You can install it on one or both sides of the stadium/arena to broadcast the advertising of your sponsors and advertisers, as the most prestigious stadiums in the world have been doing for some time. Being modular, they can be mounted on the long side or sports door of sports fields of any length.

LED Scoreboard for Sport

Thanks to a special software it is possible to transform our giant screens into scoreboards.

The advantage of using a real LED screen instead of a traditional scoreboard with only the LED numbers, is that you can easily change the arrangement of the elements and be able to show written, images or videos on the same screen, both together with the scores and in alternative to them if necessary.

Our screen models range from the Shop Screen (two meters wide and one high) to the modularity of our giant screen systems, which we can supply both indoors and outdoors. In particular, we remind you that the Tris product can be transformed both into a giant screen and into an LED field border.

FRONT SERVICE
This led screen is characterized by a full front maintenance and a lot of other innovations that make possible a number of previously unthinkable applications.

It can be fixed directly to a wall, with a unique simplicity for this product range, thanks to a modular and compact support structure designed by us.

Thanks to the possibility to act frontally and not from behind, our system allows to replace a defective element in a few minutes making all maintenance activities really simple and inexpensive.

Our Front Service led screens are only 10 cm thick and extremely lightweight (speaking of screens, these few centimeters disappear in relation to the visible surface).

Obviously this outdoor advertising screen is designed and tested to withstand the elements (IP65); also the brightness is managed dynamically to ensure maximum visibility at any time, all with a considerable reduction in power consumption.

The modularity of the system allows us to compose LED billboards of all sizes.

REAR SERVICE
Our rear-access modular led screens are the most common choice for those who need to install a signage (advertising) screen on a pole, and in some cases even on a wall, if you can leave a space on the back for maintenance.

Cabinets are commonly made of bent steel, but can also be made of aluminum; in both cases, no cabling is visible from the outside and it is possible to close the rear access doors with a key. All our cabinets are equipped with thermostatic fans and high performance switch power supplies.

The screens are available in several pixel pitch (6, 8, 10, 16, 20), always with a wide viewing angle and high contrast and brightness levels to make the image visible even in full sunlight.

FRONT & REAR SERVICE
We have further improved our Front Service line products by creating this new model that can boast double maintenance for each module, both frontal (with our revolutionary patented system) and rear, through two rear doors.

This feature allows greater flexibility, being no longer bound to specific placements due to the need for unilateral access.

Thanks to the ability to operate both front and rear access, our system allows maintenance in a few minutes regardless of the position of the screen. Also since our screen is often only 10 cm thick, it looks extremely light and non-invasive (talking about giant led billboards, these few inches disappear in relation to the visible surface).

Obviously the screen is designed and tested to withstand any weather (IP65 degree of protection); brightness is also automatically handled to ensure maximum visibility at any time, all with a remarkable reduction in consumption.

The total modularity of the system allows us to easily compose LED billboards of all sizes.

Depending on the ground height and the average viewing distance, we can offer five different resolution levels with the P6, P8, P10, P16 and P20 models.
