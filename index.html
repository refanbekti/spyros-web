ABOUT

We are a leading company in the Sport Centres Solutions field

Spyros is an International Company based in Indonesia with extensive experience in consulting, distributing and implementing equipment for Sport Centres.

The range of products and services that we provide are with full compliance with the international sport federations.

Our product range

Spyros’ portfolio includes scoreboards with video screen and led perimeter displays, fixed seating and movable seating solutions, video surveillance systems, timing devices for sports events and access control equipment.

Global reference brands

Spyros represents some of the most recognized European brands in the sector such as EURODISPLAY from Italy, FIGUERAS from Spain, ALGE from Austria, DALLMEIER from Germany and GOTSCHLICH from Austria.

Taking all together they add up more than 243 years of experience in the Sports-Stadium venues.

Complete solution

At Spyros, our highly qualified and experienced team of engineers, designers and project managers ensure that our execution fulfil all requirements, as well as its technical and economic feasibility.

We always keep the sport site owners in mind to provide them after-sales support, maintenance services and equipment warranties.
