RLS3c

×
  • The triple photocell RLS3c consists of three photocells that are integrated in one casing on top of each other.
  • It can be operated in two different modes: photocell area and single photocell use.
  • In photocell area mode, the photocell only produces an impulse if all three photocells are triggered at the same time.
  • This prevents that the photocell is triggered by e.g. an arm unintentionally passing the photocell. In this way, quite an accurate unofficial winner time is obtained.
  • This photocell is very important when a display board is used. In single photocell mode, the photocells inside the casing work as independent photocells. This means that the first one that is triggered produces a timing impulse.
